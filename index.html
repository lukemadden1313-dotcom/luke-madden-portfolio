Sideline Assistant

An AI agent built for Division III soccer coaches — pulls every recruiting signal (PDFs, club spreadsheets, microsites, group texts) into one searchable, shared workflow, and helps coaches scout, match, and compete. Designed around what I learned as a 2x All-American and Middlebury captain: D3 recruiting lives in twelve places, and no one has time to chase it. The agent does.
